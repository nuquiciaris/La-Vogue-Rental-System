Rental FAQs

How do I rent an item? +
To rent an item, simply browse our collection, select your preferred item, choose a rental date, and proceed to checkout.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and secure online payment options.
How do I return an item? +
Items should be returned to the designated drop-off location by the end of your rental period. Late fees may apply for overdue returns.
What happens if the item is damaged? +
If an item is damaged, please contact support immediately. Repair or replacement fees may apply depending on the situation.
Can I cancel my booking? +
Yes, bookings can be canceled up to 48 hours before the scheduled rental date. Cancellation fees may apply depending on the situation.
What if the size does not fit? +
If the item does not fit, please contact us immediately. We will try to arrange an exchange depending on availability.
How early should I book an item? +
We recommend booking 1–2 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons or popular event dates.
Do you accept rush rentals? +
Yes, rush rentals are available depending on item availability. Additional fees may apply.
